# Financial Consumer Complaint Analysis
## Brief Summary
Built a 3‑page Power BI dashboard analyzing complaint trends, product & issue insights, and response patterns. Highlights include top products/issues driving complaints, declining responses after 2023, and web as the primary resolution channel.

## Problem Statement
* Complaint Volume Analysis: Identify which products, sub‑products, issues, and sub‑issues generate the highest number of consumer complaints.
* Trend Detection: Track how complaint and response volumes change over time, highlighting peaks (e.g., 2022) and declines post‑2023.
* Channel Effectiveness: Evaluate which submission channels (web, phone, referral) are most used and effective in resolving complaints.
* Response Alignment: Assess whether responses align with complaint volumes, ensuring consumer issues are being addressed proportionately.
* Resolution Insights: Explore resolution outcomes (timely vs. untimely, relief provided) to measure service quality.

## Key Insights
### Executive Summary
* Complaints peaked in 2022, then declined in 2023.
* 97.61% of complaints are resolved, only 2.39% remain unresolved.
* Of resolved cases, 23.51% involved monetary relief and 8.43% non‑monetary relief.
* The web portal is the dominant complaint channel (~73%).
* California has the highest complaint volume (13,709).
* Complaints are processed quickly (avg 1.22 days), with only 3.84% untimely responses.

### Product & Issue Insight
* Hovering over product or issue bars reveals sub‑product and sub‑issue complaint distribution, giving deeper context without cluttering the page.
* Checking/Saving Accounts dominate complaints, especially Checking Accounts.
* Managing an Account is the single biggest issue, far ahead of others.
* Complaint volumes across both products and issues peak in 2022, then fall in 2023.
* The Unknown sub‑issue category is large (10,858).

### Response Insight
* Mostly all the complaints are  being resolved.
* Responses are declining as the complaints are declining after 2023.
* Most complaints are being resolved through web.
* Overall responses perftlcy align woth complaints.

### Recommendations
* Diversify revenue sources by increaing dependency on low revenue producing companies to minimize business risk.
* Standardize sales team performance strategy by aligning revenue generation, win rate, and deal closure speed across all agents, and using top performers as benchmarks for training.
* Improve quarterly consistency by analyzing Q1’s strong performance and replicating the same sales strategies across rest of the quarters.
* Optimize product performance by improving underperforming products through better targeting or pricing strategies, while scaling high-performing GTX series products. 
* Strengthen sales team efficiency balance by bridging the gap between high-volume performers and high-conversion performers to create a more balanced sales force.
